Background data allows your apps to sync even when they are not being used. Background data syncing may affect both battery life and data usage.

Selecting "All the time" will use the most battery, but will keep your phone the most up to date.

If you choose "Only when I'm using my phone", SMARTACTIONS will keep your phone up to date when it detects motion or when your display is on.

The last option, "Only when I'm using that app" saves the most battery, but your apps will only update when they are being used (no background updates).
