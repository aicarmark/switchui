Background data affects when your apps are allowed to access data, which can impact both battery and data usage.

Selecting "All the time" will use the most battery, but will keep your phone the most up to date.

If you choose "Only when I'm using my phone", SMARTACTIONS will keep your phone up to date when it detects motion or when your display is on.

The last option, "Only when I'm using that app", saves the most battery but your apps will only be updated when they are being used (no background updates).
